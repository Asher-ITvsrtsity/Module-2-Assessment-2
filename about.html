Pine City Mall, located at Leonora Dr, Ballito, Dolphin Coast, 4399, is a premier shopping destination in the heart of the vibrant coastal town of Ballito. With its modern architecture and a wide array of stores, Pine City Mall offers an exceptional shopping experience for locals and tourists alike.


Step into our mall and immerse yourself in a world of fashion, lifestyle, and entertainment. Explore a diverse range of renowned retail brands, from high-end fashion boutiques to popular household names, catering to every style and taste. Discover the latest trends, shop for exclusive designer collections, or find the perfect gift for any occasion.


At Pine City Mall, we strive to provide a pleasant and convenient shopping environment. Our spacious and well-designed layout ensures easy navigation, allowing you to browse through various stores and discover exciting new offerings. Grab a quick bite or indulge in a delicious meal at our assortment of dining options, providing a diverse selection of cuisines to satisfy any craving.
